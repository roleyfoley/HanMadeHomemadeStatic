--- title: Shipping / Delivery hero: Delivery Details ---

Orders are ONLY accepted within the ACT region (including Murrumbateman and Queanbeyan).

Pick up can be arranged from our Kitchen in Turner, ACT. Please select "Kitchen Pick-Up" at checkout. 

Delivery is available within the ACT region (including Murrumbateman and Queanbeyan) for a small flat rate cost of $5. Delivery is available Monday (2pm-5pm), Tuesday (2pm- 4pm), Wednesday (8am- 9am and 4pm-5pm), Friday (2pm-3pm) and Saturday afternoons by request. When placing your order please advise a nominated day and time within the listed options.
